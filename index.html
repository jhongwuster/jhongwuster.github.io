About WCON

Why choose WCON

Established in 2012, by a group of IT professionals, who worked amongst global top brands for 10+ years, such as HP, ShengDa. WCON currently has team over 20 people, co-located in Sydney, Shanghai and Fujian China.

Our core value stream is to "Make Your IT Happen" with a balanced cost. We offer "Design Onshore, Code Offshore" mode to save your cost.

We are truly a technical team. 60% of our resources are certified with one of the qualifications: PMP, SAFe Agile, SCJP, and Oracle.

E-Commerce Solutions

We have a solution for both entry level and senior level E-Commerce customers.
Entry Level customer:
What entry level customer wants is to get on to the online sales ASAP with less money spent. This includes sole traders and small businesses. WCON has existing and re-usable E-commerce compoment and framework that save your delopment timeline and budget.
Senior Level customer:
An E-Commerce customer will be more interested in security, stablebility, user experience and interactive promotion based on user experience to increase sales revenue. WCON has solid experience on taking all these into our design of your online services solutions.
