About WCON

Why choose WCON

Established in 2012, by a group of IT professionals, who worked amongst global top brands for 10+ years, such as HP, ShengDa. WCON currently has team over 20 people, co-located in Sydney, Shanghai and Fujian China.

Our core value stream is to "Make Your IT Happen" with a balanced cost. We offer "Design Onshore, Code Offshore" mode to save your cost.

We are truly a technical team. 60% of our resources are certified with one of the qualifications: PMP, SAFe Agile, SCJP, and Oracle.
